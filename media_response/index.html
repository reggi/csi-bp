Newspaper vs. Youtube.com

Statement:

The sheer volume of media attention given to this event is staggering. As of Nov. 29th 2010 there are 1,913 printed articles present in three leading newspapers.

The video related material generated by this event averaged 1,300 videos per month. The total amount of videos reaching a grand total of 16,500 videos in eight months.
