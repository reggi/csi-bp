Will We Ever Know?

Statement:

The attempt above displays several news sources and their competing claims on the number of dead, oiled dead, oiled alive, and saved animals (Turtles, Mammals, Birds and Reptiles) in the BP Oil Spill of 2010.

The chart shows the unreliability of such claims between the months of June to November. The truth is, no one really knows just how many animals were killed in the disaster.
